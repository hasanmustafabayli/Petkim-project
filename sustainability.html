Our Sustainability Strategy

We are forward-ready with a robust sustainability strategy. The petrochemical industry plays a key role in a sustainable life. With this belief, we take responsibility as one of the leading companies in the industry and put sustainability at the heart of our business model. We shape our sustainability strategy, which we have created in line with SOCAR Türkiye's vision and goals, according to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the Sustainability Principles Harmonization Framework determined by the Capital Markets Board (CMB), GRI standards, and stakeholder expectations. Our social, economic, and environmental repercussions are continuously monitored and managed. We provide complete infrastructure and integration studies on energy efficiency in manufacturing, occupational health and safety, process safety, resource optimization, shift to renewable energy sources, and product quality enhancement. We put people at the center of everything we do. We carry out our responsibilities to our stakeholders and society without compromising ethics, transparency, and honesty. We make every effort to protect the health and safety of our employees. We invest in social responsibility projects to enhance our social impact and benefit humankind.
